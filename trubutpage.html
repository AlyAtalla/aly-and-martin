Tribute to Mozart

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Composer and Musician

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart was a prolific and influential composer of the Classical era. Born in Salzburg, Austria in 1756, he began composing at a young age and went on to produce more than 600 works, including symphonies, operas, chamber music, and choral music. His innovations in musical form, melody, and orchestration had a significant impact on the development of Western classical music.

  • Born: January 27, 1756
  • Died: December 5, 1791
  • Contributions:
    • Composed over 600 works, including symphonies, operas, chamber music, and choral music
    • Developed new forms and structures in music
    • Influenced many composers who followed him, including Beethoven and Brahms
  • Awards: None, but widely regarded as one of the greatest composers of all time
